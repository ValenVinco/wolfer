Welcome to my website, here you will have a brief summary of everything I do and who I am. I am an Argentine artist, who resides in Bs As, I am 21 years old I am known as "Wolfer Draw", my real name is Tomas Vinco. I study visual arts at the National University of Art (UNA), I specialize in illustrations mainly of medieval fantasy, also character design and conceptual art. If you go to the "Gallery" section, you will understand what I am talking about. Do not hesitate to follow me on any of my social networks and send me any message if you have any questions, I am always attentive and aware to respond. Any other question can be directed to the "Contact Me" section and we will contact you via gmail.
